Overview

As part of the Mercedes-Benz China HR digitalization initiative, I designed and led a comprehensive digital transformation bootcamp spanning two phases across 15 months. The program targeted HR professionals across Greater China, equipping them with hands-on Power BI, Power Automate, and Microsoft 365 skills to transition from manual, spreadsheet-based workflows to self-service analytics and automated reporting.

Phase 1 focused on Power BI fundamentals through advanced analytics across 8 structured sessions, while Phase 2 introduced real-world M365 scenario-based training. The program was recognized by Mercedes-Benz China HR executive leadership and presented directly to Board Member Sabine Kohleisen.
